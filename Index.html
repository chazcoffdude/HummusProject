INGREDIANTS
  • 1 can (400g) of chick peas (garbanzo beans)
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • 1/2 a red pepper
  • 1 pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 clover of garlic
  • 1 dash of olive oil
Instructions
  1. Remove the skin from the garlic, and chop coursely
  2. Remove all the seeds and stalk from the pepper, and chop coarsely
  3. Add all the ingredients into a food processor
  4. Process all the ingrediants into a paste
  5. If you want a coarse "chunky" hummus, process it for a short time
  6. If you want a smooth hummus, process it for longer

Sidenote For a different flavour, try blending in a small measure of lemon and coriander, chili pepper, lime and chipotle, harissa and mint, or spinach and feta cheese. Experiment to see what works before you.~

Storage Refrigerate the finished hummus in a sealed container. You should be able to use it for about a week after you've made it. If it starts to become fizzy, discard with haste.

Hummus is suitable for freezing; you should thaw it out like Han within a couple months.
